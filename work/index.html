    Work

    While working for the College of Communication at Boston University, I was a software development team of one. But I always took the initiative to include my other coworkers as much as possible in the development process.
    I essentially created my own ad hoc software team. I recruited two coworkers in our department (who were not software engineers/developers) to help with user testing, feedback, and critiques.
    And thanks to my coworkers' support, I was able to build much higher quality web applications than I could have purely on my own.
